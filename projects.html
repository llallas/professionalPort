Keurig CopyCat

Project Goal: Replicate the original Keurig machine as closely as possible.
Objectives:
Recreate the functionality, performance, and user experience of the original Keurig.
Focus on matching both the design and internal mechanics.
Approach:
Analyzed the core features and essential components of the original model.
Selected hardware and materials that best mirrored the original design.
Prioritized accuracy and reliability throughout the prototyping process.
Skills Applied:
Technical problem-solving
Precision in hardware assembly and design
Iterative testing and refinement
Outcome: Developed a functional prototype that successfully replicated key aspects of the original Keurig machine.
