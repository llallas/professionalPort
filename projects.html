Travel-Fly

"Travel-Fly," an innovative website dedicated to simplifying vacation planning by providing users with an easy-to-navigate platform for exploring destinations and booking plane tickets. This project was created to assist users in discovering their next travel adventure while streamlining the decision-making and booking process. The platform features an extensive list of travel destinations, complete with curated information such as top attractions, weather, and travel tips for each location. I integrated a robust search and filter system to help users find destinations that align with their preferences, such as budget, season, or activities. Additionally, "Travel-Fly" includes a ticket booking feature that displays flight options, prices, and real-time availability through API integrations with major airlines.
